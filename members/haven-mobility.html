About Our Business

Based locally in Cwmbran, Haven Mobility is dedicated to providing premium independent living solutions across Torfaen, Newport, and the broader South Wales valleys. We specialize in supply, delivery, and personalized configurations of high-quality mobility aids.

Our team takes an open-book, honest approach to customer care. We understand that finding the right equipment is about preserving your freedom and dignity, which is why we offer relaxed, friendly consultations with zero high-pressure sales tactics.
